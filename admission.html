Admission requirement for:

Primary Level

Any child intending to join starndard one - six at Epiphany Primary School, must have completed a nursery education.

O-level

Any one intending to join form one at Epiphany Secondary School, must have completed a primary school in any recognized primary school.

A-level

Any one intending to join form five at Epiphany Secondary School, must have completed a secondary school in any recognized secondary school, with minimum C credit for each subject of aspired combination.
